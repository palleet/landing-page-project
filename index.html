Icons

Master Chief is a highly trained and augmented supersoldier, known for his exceptional combat skills and unwavering commitment to protecting humanity from extraterrestrial threats

Cortana is a sophisticated artificial intelligence system and a key character, known for her intelligence, wit, and pivotal role in assisting Master Chief in his missions.

The Warthog is a versatile, all-terrain vehicle characterized by its robust design, impressive speed and agility, and its weaponized capabilities.

The Elite is a technologically-advanced extraterrestrial race, while the Energy Sword is a double-bladed weapon that uses plasma to slice through enemies.
